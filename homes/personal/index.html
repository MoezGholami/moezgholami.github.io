Why?

As I have talked about it before, one of the unfortunate realities that we face is the faceless reality around us: boxy buildings, colorless cars, black screens, ... Even though we enjoy so many more luxuries than or predecessors, we have lost our essence in this soulless environment; as if we are a replacable masses without any identity.

My impact

To answer this challenge, I build Falomen. A startup where we listen to people's story and use cutting-edge AI and manufacturing methods to embed a touch of their soul into an intricate wearable jewelry to last for decades to come. All you need to do is to talk about you; we instantly represent your story as a design, and then we manufacture your desire into a real-life jewelry.

Let's enjoy some stories

Why?

In the era of mindless scrolling and fast fashion, when it comes to the creators of even exquisite artifacts that people use every day (i.e. luxury brands), we are usually lost by the noise of what the newest release of Rolex or the newest features of Ferrari are. Blinded by price tag wars, we forget where the people come into play. We talk about the "heritage" (brand X / artist Y was the first to make Z) but we don't talk about stories.

My impact

I've created this YouTube podcast to talk more about people and objects; plus some other things that I think I must share. I go one level deeper: I dive into the lives of stories that objects tell about people, whether it is an expensive item or not. Objects talk, objects contain a trace of human spirit, let's listen to them.
